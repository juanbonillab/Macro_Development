# Macro_Development

This repository contains Python-based empirical exercises developed for an Economic Development course. The work covers key topics in development economics, including growth accounting, human capital measurement, technology diffusion, firm heterogeneity, and structural change.

## Contents

### 📁 Human Capital & Technology
Empirical exercises based on the Manuelli-Seshadri human capital model. Recalculates human capital stocks across countries using different parameter assumptions, performs variance decompositions of cross-country income per capita differences, and explores how human capital interacts with technology adoption at the frontier.

### 📁 Firms & Aggregate Productivity
Exercises on firm size distributions and their macroeconomic implications. Estimates Pareto distributions for firm size in the US, India, Spain, and Argentina, and quantifies how differences in firm distributions account for observed gaps in Total Factor Productivity (TFP) across countries.

## Tools
Python · pandas · numpy · matplotlib · scipy
